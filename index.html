FAQ

  • info_outlineWhat is the age range of the program?

    While we think that if you can do addition you can begin to learn how to program. We are currently looking for ages 10 and up. Our plan is to have sessions for pairs of grades starting at fith and sixth. If there is demand for a younger age group we are happy to cater to that demand.

  • info_outlineWhat topics will be covered?

    The curriculum will cover the basics of frontend web technologies.
    This includes the basics of HTML, CSS and Javascript. The course schedule can easily be extended to include backend technologies, ie. databases, http servers, and other programming languages.

  • info_outlineHow long will sessions be?

    We are in the early stages of planning the schedule and organizing the curriculum, sessions at this time will be scheduled in 2 hour blocks.

  • info_outlineHow many students will be in each session?

    We are looking to keep group sizes small, so that each student will have plenty of opportunity to ask for guidance. We are aiming for 8 students per instructor.

  • info_outlineHow much will a session cost?

    Our prices are tentativly set at $75 dollars a session, and we are looking to gauge initial interest before finalizing them. Passionate students will be eligible for a discounted rate.
